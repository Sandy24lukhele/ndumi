1. About me

I am Ndumiso Dlamini from Ngwane Park. a dedicated student teacher at the University of Eswatini, pursuing a Bachelor of Education in Commerce with a focus on economics and accounting. My passion for teaching stems from a desire to inspire learners to understand the fundamental concepts of economics, particularly opportunity cost. I believe that grasping this concept is crucial for making informed decisions in both personal and professional contexts. Through engaging lessons and practical examples, I aim to equip my students with the skills they need to analyze choices and their consequences. I am committed to fostering a positive learning environment where students can explore and apply economic principles in real-world scenarios.

2. Aim of the e-Portfolio

The aim of this e-portfolio is provide learners with better understanding on the concept of opportunity cost in economics and create engaging, student-centered an interactive and conducive environment where learners can explore to real world concepts.

3. Lesson Structure

This lesson consist of a lesson plan , lecture notes on opportunity cost , Syllabus that will guide you on owhat to focus on and and link of a lesson vedio which will help you easy understand the concept.
